Satanael, God of Change. He is the chaotic lord of the celestial plane. Although his title is dark, Satanael, like all gods, are lawful entities. His creed's intent is not to spread chaos throughout the world, but to understand that order must be broken in order to bring forward positive changes. 

<b>Adds a bonus of +10 to Charisma when dealing with Chaotic characters</b>
