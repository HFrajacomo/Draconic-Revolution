Ohdos, Inferior God of Hell. Ohdos is one of the gods who decided to detach from the celestial plane and live among the living as an inferior god. It is said that he materialized as a colossal hellish creature and rules the hells. His purpose is to see death up close, even accepting multiple sacrifices in his name.

<b>Heals the user a tiny amount for every kill they get</b>
