Nigurath, the God of Corruption. His purpose is to spread corruption to the entire world. It is said Nigurath was the first undead to be able to multiply. This led to the creation of the chaotic undeads known today. Nigurath is in constant battle with Gaia.

<b>Undead characters with a Chaotic alignment were originated by Nigurath's corruption and therefore are obliged to be Necrocists</b>
<b>Allows the user to access Naturalism abilities beyond Level 10 in the Corruption subclass</b>
