Qiullia, Inferior Goddess of Luck. Bored with the celestial life, Qiullia descended to the physical plane knowing her luck wouldn't allow her to be killed. It is said that Qiullia is an adventurer, just like any other, who hunts for rare artifacts. In most scenarios, she stumbles upon the rarest of itens with astonishing ease.

<b>Grants +2 Bonus in Luck rolls and prevents Critical Failures in all Luck rolls</b>
