Eqrasil, God of Balance. He is the leader of the <i>Infernal Inquisition</i>, faction of lawful undeads that walk the hells in search of creatures intending to disrupt the balance of the world. 

<b>Undead characters with a Lawful alignment were originated by the Inquisition and therefore are obliged to be Inquisitors</b>
<b>Grants respect with figures of power in dialogues and the ability to detect creatures' alignments</b>
