Dante, God of the Underworld. Dante is the guarding of the <i>Fearscape</i>. He controls the cycle of souls through his own personal hell or the <i>Rift</i> dimension, to garantee that the cycle of life and death be complete. He is a reasonable entity, except at the presence of evil threatening the balance of the soul cycle.

<b>Increases Fire Damage inflicted by character in 20%</b>
